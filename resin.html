What is Epoxy Resin?

Epoxy resin is a versatile material used by artists to create unique and stunning artwork that has a beautiful glossy finish. Its ability to encapsulate and preserve various elements within its glass-like surface adds an element of intrigue to each creation. Whether it's encapsulationg vibrant pigments, dried flowers, or even creating intricate geode-like designs, epoxy resin opens up a world of artistic possibilities. The interplay of colors, textures, and translucency makes epoxy resin a captivating medium for both experienced artists and newcomers to the world of art.
