Meet the Artists

We are Sarah Malekyar and Daniel Prock
We are self-taught freelance artists who create art using a variety of mediums focusing mainly on epoxy resin, bead working and wire wrapping. We have honed our craft and developed a unique style that is characterized by vibrant colors and fun designs.
Epoxy resin art is a type of artwork that involves the use of epoxy resin, a synthetic material that hardens to create a clear and glossy finish. Epoxy resin is often used by artists to create a variety of different effects, such as encasing objects, creating layers, and adding vibrant colors. When used in art, the resin is typically mixed with pigments or other materials to create the desired effect. The finished product is often smooth and glossy, giving the artwork a professional and polished look. Epoxy resin art is a relatively new medium and is gaining popularity among artists who are looking for new and creative ways to express themselves.
