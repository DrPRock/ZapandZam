Watercolor Art Pricing

I love creating watercolor paintings, and I'm always happy to work with people to create a custom piece of art. My paintings are priced at 50 cents per square inch, plus the percentage added by the platform the art is sold on. So, if you were to buy a painting from me on Poshmark, I would add 20% to the final price. The size of your painting is determined by its width and height in inches. For example, a 12x16 inch painting would be 192 square inches, and would cost $96.

If you're interested in commissioning a custom watercolor painting, please contact me for a quote. I can create paintings of any size, subject matter, or style. I'm always happy to work with people to create a piece of art that they love.

Commissioned Epoxy Resin Art

We also create epoxy resin art pieces to certaint specifications. You can choose the colors and various shapes of your piece, and I will work with you to create a one-of-a-kind work of art that is perfect for your home or office.

In addition to my epoxy resin art pieces, I provide an array of custom decals to enhance their appeal. These versatile decals offer a unique way to personalize your artwork or incorporate specific themes and designs. Crafted with vinyl using a cricut machine, these decals feature beautiful silhouettes that complement vibrant and colorful epoxy backgrounds. Alternatively, we can create digital graphics that seamlessly integrate into the artwork, preserving the image's integrity and durability within the protective epoxy coating. The result is a captivating and long-lasting piece of art that truly stands out.

If you are interested in commissioning an epoxy resin art piece, please contact me to discuss your project. I would be happy to help you create a beautiful and unique piece of art that you will love for years to come.
